About Bindex Funds

Our Bindex Fund (or Bindex for short) is a private investment vehicle that is an index of the top 15 cryptocurrencies, rebalanced periodically. It is currently limited to accredited investors only with a $10,000 minimum investment, but we plan on expanding our offering to the masses in the coming time.

In your Bindex dashboard, you will purchase shares and have the ability to track your portfolio's metrics. In the future, we anticipate providing additional fund offerings, including an international investor fund.

Bindex Factors

Bindex factors in several variables in its' Top-15 selection criteria, namely:

Market Capitalization

The cryptocurrency coin's market capitalization plays a significant role in our portfolio distribution. We factor in the market cap, adjusted for inflation.

Time in Market

We only select coins that have a minimum history of at least three months in the market. Ongoing market research is applied to to ensure quality and consistency of the Bindex.

Real Price

Real Price factors in the variances in price of the coin on various exchanges, and minimizes impact that exchanges with major price deviations have on the coin's Real Price.

Trustworthiness

We are constantly performing market research every major coin that exists or is potentially going to be added into our Bindex to ensure that there is a real-world utility, roadmap, trusted team, and use-case behind them.
