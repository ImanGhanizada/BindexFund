About us

We believe that time is life's most sacred resource, and we’re on a mission to help you spend it the way you want. As promising new investment opportunities enter the market, so does the need to spend time on research. Allow us to responsibly diversify your portfolio by introducing you to a new asset - Cryptocurrency.

Our History

We were early adopters of Bitcoin, back when they were $100 a coin. The technology was promising – but still had its downfalls. The next iteration of currencies made it very obvious – the future of money movement will be heavily influenced by cryptocurrencies.

We noticed this and wanted to diversify our own portfolios, so we began investing a small portion into several cryptocurrencies. After spending hours a day on research and following a known strategy of indexing, we saw better results. Then friends and family wanted in. We realized how difficult it was for average and sophisticated investors alike to purchase and securely manage cryptocurrencies, so why not offer the service to everyone.
